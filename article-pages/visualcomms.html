Accurately predicting the future is practically impossible to do. The science fiction writers of the 1960s who imagined a world of flying cars and personalized jetpacks would surely be disappointed to see an alternative reality featuring hatchback cars and public buses. But imagine transporting someone from the 1960s to today and showing them an internet browser, an Apple iPad or a Twitter feed. If there’s one area of human life in which the revolution predicted in the 1960s has truly taken place, it’s in communications.
